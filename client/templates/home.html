This app demos some possible backoffice workflows to help iMech run smoothly

To get started just click on any item in the top bar

Possible workflow steps

    • New: New Work Order
    • Estimated: Part(s) & Labour estimated
    • Authorized: Go-ahead received from Customer
    • Scheduled: Vehicle, parts and labour scheduled to be completed
    • Started: Work incomplete
    • Finished: Work complete
    • Billed: Customer billed
    • Paid: iMech paid
